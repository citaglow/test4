    How to pay tuition

    • Upon receiving our invoice, complete your registration by making the course payment.
    • Payment should be in Korean Won (KRW currency). You can pay with a credit card (5% tax applies).
    • For wire transfer payments, you can choose to pay in Dollars or euros.
    • Wire transfer payments take 3-5 business days to be received.
    • Include wire transfer fees for both the sender and receiver in your payment.
    • Email us the bank receipt, especially if the payer's name is different from the attending student's.
