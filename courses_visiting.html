    Class Details

    • Students have the freedom to choose the location for their personalized visiting lesson in Korea.
    • While the lessons are primarily one-on-one, students can also form groups of 2 to 12 for their classes.
    • Each visiting lesson should last a minimum of 60 minutes, with an ideal duration of 90 minutes.
    • Customize the lesson term and schedule according to your preferences, and we'll work together to finalize it. For long-term students, we can create a monthly study plan.
    • To secure your enrollment, an advance payment for tuition and fees is necessary.
    • If you need to change or cancel a lesson, kindly inform us at least one day in advance. Same-day changes or cancellations are not possible, and there are no refunds for cancellations made on the same day.
    • In the event that you are unable to attend all the planned classes, the remaining sessions can be carried forward to the following month. Your next month's tuition will be adjusted accordingly.
    • Additional transportation expenses may apply for visiting teaching outside the Seoul area or in locations without public transport.

    Study Goals

    Unlocking Fluency and Cultural Understanding

    • Level 1: Introduction - Learn Korean characters, and basic sentence structure, and explore culture.
    • Level 2: Conversational Competence - Improve reading, speaking, and conversational skills, including honorifics.
    • Level 3: Effective Communication - Expand vocabulary, express various topics, and deepen cultural understanding.
    • Level 4: Social Interaction Skills - Acquire language skills for research, employment, and exploring Korean culture.
    • Level 5: Daily Situations Handling - Develop language skills for everyday situations and engage in social discussions.
    • Level 6: Fluent Social Interaction and Academic Proficiency - Cultivate language skills for formal occasions, logical expression, and real-life engagement.
    • Level 7: Advanced Academic - Refine your Korean language proficiency with advanced skills in language, research, formal writing, and critical analysis of societal issues.

    Refund policy

    • Refund Policy: 100% refund available for cancellations made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled lesson.
    • If a lesson has already started, refunds will be provided for the remaining lessons.
    • If you need to cancel or reschedule a lesson, kindly provide us with a 24-hour notice before the scheduled lesson time.
    • You have the option to reschedule lessons, up to a maximum of one-third of the total lessons within a one-month period. The rescheduled lessons should be completed within 2 months.
    • Once a lesson has been rescheduled, it cannot be further postponed or refunded.
